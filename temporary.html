VISA Information

The temporary Canada visas allow the person to stay in Canada for a specific period of time which is determined by the Canadian Consulate or the Border Services Officers (BSO)
The temporary visa can be single entry or multiple entry visa:

  • Single entry visa allows the person to only enter Canada once, stay for 6 months and then return to their home country.
  • Multiple entry visa Multiple entry visa allows the person to enter Canada multiple times until their visa expires and stay temporarily.BSO). This time is usually up to 6 months.

Visitor/Tourist visa entry to Canada

The Visitor Visa or the Tourist visa allows the holder to enter Canada for the purposes of tourism in the country.

Whether or not you need one depends on your passport and where it was issued, your nationality and your method of travel.

You may also need one if you are transiting through a Canadian airport.
